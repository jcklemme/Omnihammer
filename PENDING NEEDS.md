This can be read two ways:
Roll once: on a 4 you repair a damage result; on a 5+ you may choose to either repair a damage result OR restore a SP (5+ subsumes 4+, giving a choice)
Roll once: 4 and 5+ are separate, non-overlapping brackets (on a 4 only, repair damage; on a 5 or 6 only, restore SP — but not repair?)
