

# **SORORITAS SPECIAL RULES**

## **Shield of Faith**

*The Sisters of Battle are taught that faith is a shield stronger than any armor. Such is the power of their belief that the Emperor will protect them from mortal injury that they can shrug off blows that would fell a less devout warrior.*

* All models with the ***Shield of Faith*** special rule gain a **6+ invulnerable save** (reflected in unit profile).  
* **Deny the Witch**: A unit with ***Shield of Faith*** may attempt to **Deny the Witch** against enemy psychic powers as if it contained a psyker, even though it does not.  The unit is treated as a **Mastery Level 0** psyker for the purpose of Deny the Witch rolls only.  All normal Deny the Witch eligibility conditions apply as normal.  This does not make the unit a psyker for any other purpose.

## **Faith Points**

*The blood of martyrs is the seed of the Imperium.  Each fallen Sister strengthens the resolve of those who remain, their sacrifice a prayer answered in fire and fury.*

* The Sisters of Battle player tracks their current **Faith Points** on a counter or die visible to both players.  
* **Starting Faith Points**: The Sisters of Battle army begins the game with **zero Faith Points**.

### **Generating Faith Points**

**Faith Points** are generated immediately when any of the following triggering events occur:

| Trigger | Faith Points Gained |
| ----- | :---: |
| Start of each Sisters of Battle player turn (army must contain models with the ***Acts of Faith*** special rule) | **\+1** |
| A friendly unit with ***Acts of Faith*** is completely destroyed (reduced to 0 models or removed from play) | **\+1** |
| A friendly **CHARACTER** with ***Sacred Rites*** is slain | **\+2** |
| The Sisters of Battle player’s **Warlord** with ***Sacred Rites*** is slain  | **\+3** |

* **Faith Points** may not exceed **12** at any time.  Any **Faith Points** generated beyond 12 are lost.  
* **Faith Points** persist across turns until spent.  
* A triggering event may only generate **Faith Points** once.  If the Warlord is also a **CHARACTER** with ***Sacred Rites***, her death generates \+3 **Faith Points**, not \+3 and \+2.  
* Units without ***Acts of Faith*** do not generate **Faith Points** when destroyed.

## **Acts of Faith**

*When the faithful cry out to the Emperor in their darkest hour, sometimes He answers.*

This unit is eligible to invoke Acts of Faith when conditions are met.

### **Who Can Invoke**

A unit may attempt an **Act of Faith** if it meets **both** of the following condition:

* The unit has the ***Acts of Faith*** special rule.  
* The unit contains at least one model with ***Sacred Rites*** OR the unit contains a model carrying a **Simulacrum Imperialis**.

Units that do not meet both conditions may not attempt **Acts of Faith**, regardless of available **Faith Points**.

### **How to Invoke**

To attempt an **Act of Faith**:

1. **Declare:** At the timing specified by the Act, declare which **Act of Faith** the unit is attempting.  
2. **Spend:** Reduce the **Faith Point** total by 1\.  If the army has 0 **Faith Points**, no Act may be attempted.  
3. **Test:** Roll 2D6.  If the result is **equal or less than** the invoking unit’s Leadership value, the Act is successful and takes immediate effect.  If the result exceeds the unit’s Leadership, the Act fails but the **Faith Point** is still spent.

### **Restrictions**

The following rules apply to **Acts of Faith**:

* A unit may only attempt one **Act of Faith** per phase, regardless of success or failure.  
* The same **Act of Faith**  may be attempted by **different units** in the same phase (each paying 1 FP separately).  
* A unit may attempt different Acts in different phases of the same turn.  
* **Acts of Faith** are **not** psychic powers.  They may not be denied by **Deny the Witch** or any anti-psychic ability.  They are not affected by psychic hoods, Shadow in the Warp, or similar effects.  
* A unit that is **FALLING BACK** may not attempt an **Act of Faith**.

### **Modifiers to the Leadership Test**

The modifiers listed here only modify Leadership for the purpose of ***Acts of Faith*** tests, they do not affect Morale tests or any other Leadership-based test.  Leadership may not be modified above 10\.

| Condition | Modifier |
| ----- | :---: |
| Unit contains a **Simulacrum Imperialis** | **\+1 Ld** |
| Unit contains a model with ***Sacred Rites*** AND contains a **Simulacrum Imperialis** | **\+2 Ld** |
| Unit is below half strength | **\+1 Ld** |

## **The Six Acts of Faith**

### **Hand of the Emperor**

*The Emperor lends His terrible strength to the faithful, their blows striking with force far beyond mortal muscle.*

* **Phase:** Either player’s Assault Phase; declare before rolling To Hit for the invoking unit.  
* **Effect:** Until the end of the phase, all models in the unit add **\+2** to their Strength characteristic.  However, all models in the unit strike at **Initiative 1** for duration of the phase.  
* **MAY NOT BE COMBINED WITH THE PASSION IN THE SAME PHASE ON THE SAME UNIT**

### **Divine Guidance**

*The Emperor guides the aim of the faithful.  Bolts find weak points in armour.  Blades slip between joints.  Every shot, every blow, strikes true.*

* **Phase:** Own Shooting phase or either player’s Assault phase; declare before rolling To Wound for the invoking unit.  
* **Effect:** Until the end of the phase, **To Wound rolls of 6** made by the invoking unit are resolved at **AP1** for ranged attacks or count as ***Power Weapon*** attacks in melee.  Against vehicles, **Armor Penetration rolls of 6** add an additional **\+D3** to the penetration result.

### **Spirit of the Martyr**

*The faithful are wreathed in golden light.  Blows that should slay glance harmlessly aside, deflected by an unseen hand.*

* **Phase:** Enemy Shooting phase or either player’s Assault Phase; declare at the start of the phase, before any attacks are resolved against the invoking unit.  
* **Effect:** Until the end of the phase, all models in the invoking unit gain an **invulnerable save equal to their armor save value**.  This invulnerable save does not stack with any source that improves invulnerable saves.

### **The Passion**

*The fury of the Emperor fills the hearts of the faithful.  They strike with preternatural speed, blades lashing out before the enemy can react.*

* **Phase:** Either player’s Assault phase; declare before resolving the melee engagement the invoking unit is involved in.  
* **Effect:** Until the end of the phase, all models in the invoking unit add **\+2** to their Initiative characteristic.  
* **MAY NOT BE COMBINED WITH HAND OF THE EMPEROR IN THE SAME PHASE ON THE SAME UNIT**

### **Light of the Emperor**

*In their darkest hour, the faithful find the light of the Emperor burning within them.  Fear is burned away.  Doubt is ash.  Only duty remains.*

* **Phase:** Any phase; declare immediately when the invoking unit is required to take a Morale test (Break Test, Casualty Test, or Pinning Test), or at the start of any phase  
* **Effect:** Until the end of the turn, the invoking unit gains ***Fearless***.  If the unit is currently **Falling Back**, it immediately regroups without needing to make a Regroup test, regardless of any modifiers or restrictions that would normally prevent regrouping.  The unit may then act normally.  
* **Falling Back:** **Light of the Emperor** may target units that are **Falling Back**.  This is an exception to the rules that normally prevent units that are **Falling Back** from attempting **Acts of Faith**.

### **Martyrdom**

*Even in death, the faithful serve.  A fallen heroine rises one final time, wreathed in holy light, to strike down the enemies of the Emperor before her spirit ascends.*

* **Phase:** Any phase; declare immediately when a friendly **CHARACTER** with ***Sacred Rites*** is slain (reduced to 0 wounds).  
* **Effect:** The slain **CHARACTER** is not removed from play.  Instead, she is restored with 1 wound remaining and may continue to act normally until the next end of player turn of the controlling player.  At the next end of turn of the controlling player, the **CHARACTER** is removed from play as a casualty.  
* **RESTRICTIONS:**  
  * **Once Per Game:** Martyrdom may only be successfully invoked once, regardless of how many characters are slain.  
  * Martyrdom generates **no additional Faith Points** when the character is finally removed at the end of the turn (she has already been counted as slain for **Faith Point** generation purposes).  
  * If the Martyrdom Leadership test fails, the **CHARACTER** is removed as normal and the **Faith Point** is spent.  The once-per-game restriction is **not** consumed by a failed attempt; only a successful invocation counts.

## **Sacred Rites**

*This character carries the faith of the Sisterhood and can focus the devotion of those around her into miraculous acts.*

A **CHARACTER** with ***Sacred Rites*** enables any unit she is part of or has joined to attempt **Acts of Faith** (provided the unit also has the ***Acts of Faith*** special rule).

## **Order Convictions**

A model with this special rule gains all the effects of the chosen Order for the detachment.  It may also invoke the listed additional **Act of Faith** in the Order’s rules in addition to the Six Acts of Faith.

# **ORDERS OF THE SORORITAS**

## **ORDER OF OUR MARTYRED LADY**

*In death, duty.  In sacrifice, strength.  We are the blood of the martyrs, and our blood is the Emperor’s fire.*

### **The Blood of Martyrs**

* When a unit with the ***Order Convictions*** special rule for this Order is below its starting strength (has lost one or more models), all models in that unit gain \+1 BS (to a maximum of BS5) and \+1 WS (to a maximum of WS5).  
* When any unit with this Order's ***Order Convictions*** is completely destroyed, the Sororitas player gains \+1 Faith Point **in addition to the standard Faith Point** generation from the Acts of Faith rules. This stacks with the normal \+1 FP for a unit with Acts of Faith being destroyed (total \+2 FP for a destroyed Martyred Lady unit with Acts of Faith).

### **Unto Death**

Units with this Order's ***Order Convictions*** may not voluntarily Fall Back from close combat. If they lose a melee engagement and fail their Break Test, they **Fall Back** as normal, but they may never choose to voluntarily fail a Break Test or use any rule that allows voluntary disengagement from close combat.

### **Act of Faith: Vengeance of the Fallen**

* **Phase:** Either player’s Assault phase; declare before rolling to hit for the invoking unit.  
* **Effect:** Until the end of the phase, the invoking unit gains a number of **bonus attacks** equal to the number of **models the unit has lost** since the start of the game.  These bonus attacks are distributed as evenly as possible across surviving models.  Bonus attacks use the model’s standard melee weapons and profile.
