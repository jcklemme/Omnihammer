# **UNIT PROFILES**

#### Colonel ‘Iron Hand’ Straken

|  | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld | Save | Inv | Type |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Colonel ‘Iron Hand’ Straken | 5 | 4 | 6 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 9 | 3+ | \- | Infantry (Character) |

One **Company Command Squad** in an army may replace its Company Commander with Colonel ‘Iron Hand’ Straken  
---

Colonel ‘Iron Hand’ Straken has the following rules  
***Cold Steel and Courage:*** This model, and all friendly units from Codex: Imperial Guard within 6” of it, have the **Counter-Attack** and **Furious Charge** special rules  
***Fearless***  
***Gung-ho:*** This model must always issue and accept a challenge whenever possible  
***Monster Hunter***  
***Senior Officer***  
***Smash***  
***Voice of Command***

---

Colonel ‘Iron Hand’ Straken is equipped with the following Wargear:

Flak Armor  
Plasma Pistol  
Shotgun  
Close Combat Weapon  
Frag Grenades  
Refractor Field

Options:

* Colonel ‘Iron Hand’ Straken must take the **Implacable Determination** Warlord Trait if they are selected as an army’s Warlord

#### Color Sergeant Kell

|  | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld | Save | Inv | Type |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Color Sergeant Kell | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 2 | 4 | 2 | 8 | 4+ | \- | Infantry (Character) |

If a **Company Command Squad** includes **Lord Castellan Creed,** then it may replace one **Veteran** with **Color Sergeant Kell**  
---

Color Sergeant Kell has the following rules:  
***Listen Up, Maggots\!:*** If Kell is in the same unit as Creed, Leadership tests for orders issued by Creed can be taken on Kell’s leadership, not that of the ordered unit.  
***Look Out – Arghh\!:***  
***Sworn Protector***

---

Color Sergeant Kell is equipped with the following Wargear:

Carapace Armor  
Laspistol  
Power Fist  
Power Sword  
Frag Grenades  
Regimental Standard\*  
\*(No Veteran may carry a Regimental Standard in a Company Command Squad that includes Kell)
