### Core Codicies

All Core Codicies are meant to be played together with the Omnihammer Ruleset.  You should not limit usage of any of these in your game group.  If you notice that something doesn’t feel fun or there is a major imbalance you are urged to please use the Google Docs comment function and leave feedback.  We can only play so many games and field so many armies, so we count on other groups to let us know if they notice something fucky.    
You may notice units that appear in lore but have never received a model or rules.  This is due to the philosophy that Games Workshop needs the community a whole hell of a lot more than the community needs Games Workshop.  We **strongly** encourage you to 3D print, kitbash, use proxies, or scratch-build whatever brings you joy.  They are *your* dudes after all.

If a Codex is YELLOW it means it is currently being worked on

### Auxiliary Codicies

An Auxiliary Codex is meant to represent an out-there or one-off force.  These may not be canon and you are more than welcome to limit or prohibit the usage of some or all of these in your gaming group.
