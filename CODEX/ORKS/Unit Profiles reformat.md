|  | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld | Save | Inv | Type |

| Runtherd | 3 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 2 | 5 | 6+ | \- | Infantry (Character) |

A Gretchin Unit consists of 10–40 Gretchin models.  
---

Gretchin have the following rules:  
***Expendable:*** When a unit with this rule is destroyed, friendly units within 6" do not need to take a CASUALTY TEST or any other MORALE TEST as a result. Additionally, the destruction of a unit with this rule does not count toward determining whether enemy victory conditions related to destroying units have been met.  
***Weedy Little Gitz:*** Gretchin models gain +1 to any cover saves conferred by terrain. Gretchin models do not receive a cover save while in the open.  
***Runtherd's Discipline*** *(Runtherd only)*: While a Runtherd is alive in the unit, when the unit fails a BREAK TEST, CASUALTY TEST, PINNING TEST, or REGROUP TEST, the controlling player may immediately remove a number of Gretchin models from the unit equal to the amount by which the test was failed. If sufficient Gretchin are removed in this way, the test counts as passed. If there are not enough Gretchin remaining in the unit to cover the difference, the test fails as normal and the remaining Gretchin may not be sacrificed for a partial effect.  
***Squig Hound Discipline*** *(Runtherd with Squig Hound only)*: While the Runtherd has a Squig Hound, the Runtherd's Discipline rule is modified — instead of removing Gretchin equal to the amount the test was failed by, the controlling player removes only D3 Gretchin models. If the D3 result is less than the amount the test was failed by, the test still counts as passed.

---

Grot Blasta  
Close combat weapon

Grot-Prod  

Options:  
* The unit may include one Runtherd	X pts  
* A Runtherd may take a Squig Hound	X pts

#### Mekboy Junka

|  | BS | FAV | SAV | RAV | SP | Type |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---- |
| Mekboy Junka | 2 | 11 | 11 | 10 | 1 | Vehicle (Tank, Fast, Open-Topped, Transport) |

A Mekboy Junka Unit consists of 1 Mekboy Junka model. A Mekboy Junka may only be taken as a DEDICATED TRANSPORT for a Big Mek.  
---

Mekboy Junkas have the following rules:  
***Klan Kultur*** ***Ramshackle*** ***Turbo-Charger:*** At the start of the movement phase, the controlling player may declare that the Mekboy Junka is using its Turbo-Charger. Roll a D6: on a 1, the vehicle is immediately Immobilized (the engine blows). On a 2+, the vehicle adds +6" to all movement mode distances this turn. A vehicle that has been Immobilized by the Turbo-Charger may be repaired as normal.  
***Da Mek's Ride (Shokk Attack Gun Mishaps):*** If the Mekboy Junka is equipped with a turret-mounted Shokk Attack Gun and a result on the Shokka Mishap table would normally affect "the firer" or "the bearer," the effect is applied to the Mekboy Junka instead. Results that would remove the firer from play instead inflict a Penetrating Hit on the vehicle. Results that would move or teleport the firer instead move or teleport the entire vehicle (and any embarked passengers). Any models disembarking involuntarily as a result of a mishap must take a PINNING TEST.  

**Transport Capacity:** 10 models. Mega Armour models count as two models. If the vehicle is equipped with a 'Speshul Bit', the transport capacity is reduced to 6.  
**Fire Points:** 4 models may fire from the vehicle (Open-Topped).  
**Access Points:** One access point on each side, one at the rear.  

---

Mekboy Junkas are equipped with the following Wargear:  
Three Big Shootas (pintle-mounted)  
Grot Riggers
