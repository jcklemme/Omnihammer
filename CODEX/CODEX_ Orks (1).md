

# **ORKS SPECIAL RULES**

## **Mob Rule**

*Orks don’t run because they’re scared; they run because nobody’s watchin’.  Or sometimes they don’t run at all.  Sometimes they do something much, much worse.*

**Mob Rule** modifies how Ork units handle failed **MORALE TESTS** (**BREAK TESTS, CASUALTY TESTS, PINNING TESTS,** & **REGROUP TESTS**).  Follow these steps when an Ork unit with ***Mob Rule*** is required to make a **MORALE TEST**:

1. **DETERMINE LEADERSHIP VALUE**: The unit may use the number of models currently in the unit as its Leadership value, up to a maximum of 10\.  Alternatively, the unit may use its base Leadership from its **DATASHEET** profile.  Choose whichever value you wish to use.  
2. **TAKE THE MORALE TEST:** Roll 2d6 and compare it to the Leadership value determined in Step 1\.  If the result is equal or less than the Leadership value, proceed no further.  
3. **IF FAILED, ROLL ON THE MOB RULE TABLE:** Do **NOT** apply the normal consequences of the failed test (falling back, becoming pinned, remaining broken, etc.).  Instead, roll 1D20 and consult the **MOB RULE TABLE** below.  The result of the table replaces all normal consequences of the failed test, regardless of which type of Moral Test was failed.

## **Klan Kultur**

Each Ork Klan has a distinct kultur that shapes how its boyz fight, what they value, and what they’re rubbish at.  When assembling your army, you must choose one of the Klanz listed in the **KLANZ OF DA ORKS** chapter.  Each Detachment in your army may only include Orks from a single Klan.  An Ork model the the ***Klan Kultur*** special rule gains the benefits and drawbacks conferred by the detachment’s chosen Klan.  Models without ***Klan Kultur*** (such as Gretchin) are not affected.

## **WAAAGH\! ENERGY**

The Ork player tracks their current **WAAAGH\! ENERGY** on a counter or die visible to both players.

### **Generating WAAAGH\! ENERGY**

**WAAAGH\! ENERGY** is generated immediately when any of the following triggering events occur:

| Trigger | Energy Gained |
| ----- | :---: |
| An Ork unit successfully completes a charge move against an enemy unit | **\+1** |
| An enemy unit is destroyed (reduced to 0 models or removed from play) by Ork Attacks | **\+D3** |
| An enemy HQ unit is destroyed | **\+3** |
| An enemy Warlord is destroyed | **\+5** |

* **WAAAGH\! ENERGY** may not exceed 20 at any time.  Any **WAAAGH\! ENERGY** generated beyond 20 is lost.  
* **WAAAGH\! ENERGY** persists across turn until spent.  
* A triggering event may only generate **WAAAGH\! ENERGY** once.  If an enemy HQ unit is destroyed, it only generates \+3 **WAAAGH\! ENERGY**, not \+3 and \+D3

### **Spending WAAAGH\! ENERGY**

To spend **WAAAGH\! ENERGY**, the controlling player declares the effect and deducts the cost during the indicated phase.  The following rules govern spending:

* A player may spend **WAAAGH\! ENERGY** on multiple different effects in the same turn  
* Unless otherwise noted, each effect may only target a single unit per use.  The same effect may be used on different units in the same turn by paying the cost separately for each.  
* Effects with a “once per game” restriction may only be used once, regardless of how many times the cost could be paid.  
* If a cost cannot be fully paid, the effect may not be used.  
* Effects take place immediately upon spending the **WAAAGH\! ENERGY**, unless the effect specifies a different timing.  
* Effects may never target a unit containing models with the ***Expendable*** special rule.  
* **WAAAGH\! ENERGY** may be spent during either player’s turn.

## **Warboss Ambitions**

At the start of the game, before deployment, the Ork player secretly selects one Warboss Ambition from the table below and writes it down. Do not reveal the chosen Ambition to your opponent.

* An Ambition's condition is checked at the moment the triggering event occurs. If the condition is met, the Ambition activates immediately — declare it to your opponent and apply the effect.  
* Once activated, an Ambition remains active for the rest of the game unless the effect specifies otherwise.  
* If the **WARLORD** is removed as a casualty before the Ambition activates, the Ambition is lost and has no effect.  
* A Warlord may only have one Ambition.

## **Ramshackle**

***Ramshackle:*** When a vehicle with this rule suffers a result on the **VEHICLE DAMAGE TABLE**, roll a D6. On a 6, the damage result is ignored entirely — the shot passes clean through the vehicle's haphazard construction without hitting anything important. This roll is made after all modifiers to the damage result have been applied, but before **STRUCTURE POINTS** are spent. ***Ramshackle*** may not ignore an **EXPLODES\!** Result.

## **Ork Weapon Mountin’**

***Ork Weapon Mountin':*** Ork vehicles do not have standardized weapon mounting positions. The placement of weapons on an Ork vehicle model is determined by the modeler. The **FIRING ARC** of each weapon is determined by its physical position on the model, following **WYSIWYG** principles — a weapon mounted on the front of the vehicle fires in the front arc, a weapon on the side fires in that side's arc, a turret-mounted weapon may fire in all arcs, and so on. If a weapon's placement is ambiguous, both players must agree on its firing arc before the game begins. 

* ***Ork Weapon Mountin’*** applies to all Ork Vehicles, it is not listed in the Unit Profile.

## **Expendable**

***Expendable:*** A unit with this rule is subject to the following restrictions:

* The destruction of a unit with this rule does not count toward enemy victory conditions related to destroying units.  
* A unit with this rule may not control or contest **OBJECTIVES**.  
* A unit with this rule may not benefit from **WAAAGH\! ENERGY** spending effects.  
* A unit with this rule may not benefit from **WARBOSS AMBITIONS** effects.  
* A unit with this rule does not generate **WAAAGH\! ENERGY** when it destroys an enemy unit or completes a charge

## **Reckless Driver**

***Reckless Driver:*** This vehicle may perform **TANK SHOCK** and **RAM** attacks following all rules described in the **TANK SHOCK & RAMMING** section of the Core Rules, despite not having the **Tank** vehicle type.  When calculating the Strength of a **RAM** hit, this vehicle counts as a **Tank** for the purpose of the \+1 Strength modifier.  Additionally, this vehicle may move over friendly models during its movement.  Friendly models moved over are considered **DRIVEN OVER** as described in the **TANK SHOCK** rules.  This is an exception to the Core Rules restriction of Tank Shock, Ram, and moving over friendly models.

# **KLANZ OF DA ORKS**

# **GOFFS**

*Da ‘Ardest Boyz; "We'z da biggest and da strongest\! If you ain't fightin', you ain't nuffin'\!"*

## **Signature Rule**

### **Born Scrappers**

Goffs models gain \+1 WS when locked in close combat with an enemy unit whose majority WS is higher than the Goffs model's base WS. Additionally, when a Goffs unit successfully completes a charge move, all models in the unit gain \+1 Attack for the remainder of that player turn. This bonus is cumulative with the standard \+1 Attack for charging, for a total of \+2 Attacks on the turn the unit charges.

## **Drawback**

### **‘Oo Needz Gunz?**

All Goffs models suffer \-1 BS (to a minimum of 1). This penalty does not apply to models with a base BS of 0\.

# **EVIL SUNZ**

*Da Red Onez; "RED WUNZ GO FASTA\! Dat's not just a sayin', it's SCIENCE\!"*

## **Signature Rule**

### **Speed Freeks**

Evil Sunz **VEHICLE** models gain \+2" to all movement mode distances (cumulative with the Red Paint Job vehicle upgrade). Evil Sunz **INFANTRY** models gain \+1" to their normal movement distance and may fire Assault weapons at their full BS after making an advance move (instead of snap fire). Evil Sunz **VEHICLE** models that moved at flat out speed may fire snap shots during the shooting phase. **This is an exception to the normal vehicle rules, which prohibit shooting after moving at flat out speed.**

## **Drawback**

### **Can’t Stop Won’t Stop**

When an Evil Sunz unit fails a **BREAK TEST** or **CASUALTY TEST**, the unit falls back toward the controlling player's board edge at its full normal movement distance \+D6", rather than the standard 2D6" fall back distance. Additionally, Evil Sunz units may not voluntarily go to ground and may not use the **TAKE COVER\!** Reaction.

# **BAD MOONZ**

*Da Richest Gitz; "We got da biggest gunz coz we got da most teef\!"*

## **Signature Rule**

### **More Dakka**

When a Bad Moons model makes a ranged attack, the AP value of each weapon it fires is improved by 1 (e.g., AP6 becomes AP5, AP5 becomes AP4). A weapon with AP1 cannot be further improved. A weapon with AP— is not affected. Additionally, each Bad Moons unit may re-roll one failed To Hit roll per shooting phase.

## **Drawback**

### **Flash Gitz**

When a Bad Moons unit destroys an enemy unit during the shooting phase, the Bad Moons unit must immediately take a **LEADERSHIP TEST**. If the test is failed, the unit may not declare charges during the assault phase of the same player turn.

# **DEATHSKULLS**

*Da Lucky Gitz; "Iz not stealin', it's redistributin'\! Gork sez it's mine now."*

## **Signature Rule**

### **Lucky Blue Gitz**

Deathskulls models gain a **6+ invulnerable save**. Additionally, once during each of the Ork player's player turns, the controlling player may re-roll a single die roll made for or against any one Deathskulls unit. This re-roll may be applied to any type of die roll (To Hit, To Wound, saving throws, morale tests, damage table rolls, etc.). The re-roll must be declared before any subsequent dice are rolled.

## **Drawback**

### **Sticky Fingerz**

At the end of the movement phase, each Deathskulls unit that is within 3" of a destroyed vehicle wreck, crashed flyer, or dead **MONSTROUS CREATURE** model must take a **LEADERSHIP TEST**. If the test is failed, the unit is occupied looting for the remainder of the player turn — it may not shoot in the shooting phase and may not declare charges in the assault phase.

# **BLOOD AXES**

*Da Tak-Tik-Ul Gitz; "Tactics iz not un-orky\! It's just krumpin' smarter\!"*

## **Signature Rule**

### **Taktikal Jeenyus**

Blood Axes units gain ***Scouts*** and ***Move Through Cover***. Additionally, after making an advance move, Blood Axes models may fire Assault weapons at their full BS (instead of snap fire) and may fire Rapid Fire weapons at snap fire (instead of not being permitted to fire). Heavy weapons may not be fired after advancing, even by Blood Axes. **This is an exception to the normal advance move rules.**

## **Drawback**

### **Da Plan Iz Da Plan**

At the start of the game, after deployment but before the first turn, the Blood Axes player must secretly write down a **Battle Plan** consisting of one priority target unit type (e.g., "enemy HQ," "enemy Heavy Support," "enemy Troops") and one priority board region (e.g., "left flank," "center," "right flank," defined by dividing the table into thirds lengthwise). Blood Axes units that shoot at or charge units matching the priority target type, or that are within the priority board region, operate normally. Blood Axes units that shoot at or charge units that do not match the priority target type AND are outside the priority board region suffer \-1 BS and \-1 WS for that attack.

# **SNAKEBITES**

*Da Old Wayz; "We don't need no stinkin' machines\! Da old ways is da best ways\!"*

### **Force Organization**

A Snakebites Detachment may take Squig Packs as Troops.

## **Signature Rule**

### **‘Ard As Nails**

Snakebites **INFANTRY** and **CAVALRY** models gain \+1 Toughness. Additionally, all Snakebites models gain ***Feel No Pain (6+)***. A model that already has ***Feel No Pain*** from another source improves it by 1 instead (to a maximum of ***Feel No Pain (4+)***).

## **Drawback**

### **No Trust In Mekwork**

Snakebites **VEHICLE** models (excluding Walkers and Chariots) suffer \-1 BS. Additionally, a Snakebites detachment must include less **VEHICLE** units (excluding Chariots) than the total number of **INFANTRY** and **CAVALRY** units in the same detachment. This restriction is checked at list-building time.

# **FREEBOOTERZ**

*Da Flashest Pirates; "We fight for whoever's got da most teef\! And if dey ain't got enough teef, we take 'em anyway\!"*

## **Signature Rule**

### **Showoffz**

Whenever a friendly Freebooterz unit destroys an enemy unit, every other friendly Freebooterz unit that can draw line of sight to any model in the destroyed unit (at the moment of its destruction) gains \+1 BS and \+1 WS until the end of the current phase. If multiple enemy units are destroyed in the same phase, this bonus stacks for each destruction event, to a maximum of \+3 BS and \+3 WS.

## **Drawback**

### **Pay Day Or No Way**

At the start of each Ork player turn, beginning with the second battle round, check whether any enemy units were destroyed during the previous battle round (both player turns). If no enemy units were destroyed, every Freebooterz unit in the army must take a **CASUALTY TEST** with a \-2 Leadership modifier.
